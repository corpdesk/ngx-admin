ILCU Foundation: Call for Expression of Interest (EoI)

by Accosca Admin | EOI, Updates Jul 22, 2020

The Irish League of Credit Unions Foundation (ILCUF) is the charitable arm of the Irish credit union movement. Its mission is to empower individuals and communities in the developing world to fight poverty and financial exclusion through ethical savings and loans.

ILCUF considers training and education a key part of our provision of technical assistance. Training and competency development is essential for CU movement survival and growth. To this end, ILCUF is developing a CU training curriculum for CUs, apexes and regulators to help engender best practice in CU development. The CU curriculum will feature both core and supplementary courses for CU leaders in the area of governance and CU staff in the area of operations. All training will be delivered via classroom based training and should be engaging using a variety of interactive and innovative training methodologieswhich cater with adult learning needs in-country.
